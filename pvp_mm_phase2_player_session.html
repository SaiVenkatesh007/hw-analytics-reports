Key findings

What Phase 2 adds on top of Phase 1

Center is healthy: Median rolling close ~56% matches Phase 1 per-user baseline. Most players with enough history see competitive games.
Blowout tail = positive blowout streaks: Match-level 23.8% positive vs 4.1% negative blowout. Rolling-25: 9.2% pos cluster vs 0.06% neg cluster (40 users). Unsigned 13.8% is almost entirely wins-big, not stompings.

Chronic mismatch is not the story: Only 0.5% below 20% close over 25 matches.
Sessions are not the problem: Zero-close marathons 0.9% of 8+ sessions. Retention extension parked — in-window next-match ≈100%.

Methodology

Scope, filters, and metric definitions

Data scope

    Metric definitions

    • Close match: Batting first → margin ≤5 runs; bowling first → ≤3 balls remaining (18-ball format for Rookie 1–2, else 30).
    • One-sided / blowout: Batting first → margin ≥15 runs; bowling first → ≥12 balls remaining.
    • Rolling-25: Each user's last 25 human PvP matches in the window, chronological.
    • Rolling close %: % of those 25 matches that were close.
    • Blowout cluster: >40% of last 25 matches were one-sided (unsigned).
    • Negative blowout: One-sided loss — got stomped.
    • Positive blowout: One-sided win — dominant victory.
    • Power ratio: opp_power / team_power — <1 means you're stronger; <0.85 = much stronger.
    • Positive blowout cluster: >40% of last 25 matches were dominant wins — the actionable rolling tail.
    • Negative blowout cluster: >40% stomped over last 25 — vanishingly rare (0.06%).
    • Next-match retention: Parked — in-window metric ≈100%; needs same-day or next-session definition.
    • Chronic mismatch: <20% of last 25 matches were close (investigation threshold; almost nobody hits it).
    • Session: Consecutive matches with ≤30 min gap between them.
    • Session frustration: 8+ matches in one session with zero close matches.
    • Below-min session: Session length expects a minimum close count (e.g. 8–14 matches → need ≥2 close) and didn't meet it.
    • Activity tier: match_count / active_days — Casual ≤5, Regular 6–15, High-freq 15+.
    • Power bracket: calibratedTotalInternalStrength at match time — 0–50k, 50k–75k, 75k–100k, 100k+.

    Caveats

    • pvp_extra_data power available from Jun 15 — window starts Jun 15, not Jun 1.
    • Only 33% of PvP users reach 25 matches in 16 days — rolling metrics exclude casual/low-volume players.
    • teams2 inner join maps team_id → user_id; drops non-real-user teams (~35% of raw rows).
    • Small-n cells (e.g. high-freq × Masterful n=60 rolling-25) should not drive decisions alone.
