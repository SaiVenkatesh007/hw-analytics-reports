Winstreak A/B Read · May 15 – Jun 21

3-Win Streak vs 4-Win Streak Reversion

Base  1 in 5 wins reverts to normal MM (4-win streak)  ·  Test  1 in 4 wins reverts to normal MM (3-win streak)

Read: Test case triggers normal MM more frequently (1.15 vs 0.89 normal matches/day) — working as intended. Real-user WR is lower in Test (58% vs 63%) — expected, as more frequent reversion means more hard matches. NDR is virtually identical (72.3% vs 72.4%) — more hard matches are not driving whales away. Raw monetisation favours Base, but this is driven by a small number of highrollers. Once excluded (>₹10k, >₹15k cuts), ARPU and ARPPU are flat to marginally better in Test, with Conv% ahead in Test at every cut. The slight dip in matches/day (3.76 vs 3.91) is the one metric to continue monitoring.

Winstreak A/B Read · Jun 15 – Jun 29

3-Win Streak vs 4-Win Streak Reversion — Extended Read

Base  1 in 5 wins  ·  Test  1 in 4 wins  ·  Separate from the May 15 – Jun 21 read — same experiment, longer window.

Read: MDAU is essentially flat (3.70 vs 3.61) — the engagement gap is narrowing vs earlier windows. NDR is up in both arms (~77%) with no meaningful cross-arm delta. Real-user WR is lower in Test (65% vs 60%) as expected with more frequent normal MM. MM-whale monetisation is flat once highrollers are excluded (ARPU ₹167 vs ₹171 excl. >₹10k).
