PvP Matchmaking — Phase 2 Player & Session Read
Human vs human ARENAS · rolling-25 + sessions + extensions · Jun 15–30, 2026
Dashboard: https://saivenkatesh007.github.io/hw-analytics-reports/pvp_mm_phase2_player_session.html

*Context: Phase 2 — player rolling quality + session experience (builds on Phase 1 baseline)*
   - Window: Jun 15–30 (pvp_extra_data power available from Jun 15)
   - Rolling-25 = each user's last 25 human PvP matches in window
   - power_ratio = opp_power / team_power (<1 = you're stronger)
   - Extensions: signed blowout, power-ratio close %, next-match retention (latter not actionable — see §5)

*1. Median rolling experience is healthy — blowout streaks are the real tail*
   - 2.8M unique matches; 220K PvP users; 72K users with ≥25 matches (33%)
   - Match-level: 55.5% close, 27.8% one-sided (aligned with Phase 1)
   - Median rolling close %: 56% (rolling-25 eligible users)
   - Unsigned blowout cluster: 13.8% of rolling-25 users (~10K users)
   - Chronic mismatch: 0.5% only — too rare to headline

*2. Blowout cluster concentrates in high-frequency play and 75k+ power*
   - Casual: 12.3% blowout cluster, 56% median rolling close
   - Regular: 16.0%, 52%
   - High-freq (15+/day): 26.0%, 48% — ~1,119 rolling-25 users
   - 75k–100k power: 25.5% blowout cluster; 100k+: 20.7%
   - High-freq × Supreme+: 30.8% blowout cluster (n=312)

*3. Session marathon frustration is not a product problem*
   - 89% of sessions are 1–3 matches; 8+ match sessions are 1.2% of all sessions
   - Zero-close marathons (8+ matches, 0 close): 0.9% of long sessions — negligible
   - Below-min in 8+ sessions: 5.4% — real but thin base; not the lead story

*4. Extensions — signed blowout reframes the blowout tail*
   - Match-level split: 4.1% negative blowout (stomped) vs 23.8% positive blowout (dominant win) — not 50/50
   - Rolling-25: negative blowout cluster 0.06% (40 users) · positive blowout cluster 9.2% · unsigned 13.8%
   - Median rolling over last 25: 4% negative blowout vs 20% positive blowout
   - High-freq: pos cluster 18.1% (neg 0.2%); 75k–100k: pos cluster 20.8%; High-freq × Supreme+: pos 22.4%, unsigned 30.8%
   - **Reframe:** unsigned blowout cluster is almost entirely positive-blowout streaks — monitor pos cluster in high-freq / 75k+

*5. Extensions — close % by power ratio (MM calibration)*
   - Per-user avg close overall: 58.7%
   - Parity band 0.95–1.0: 56.3% close; lowest when much stronger (<0.85 → 47.0% close, 37% pos blowout)
   - Undertiered (1.15+ opp stronger): 63.8% close but 6.7% negative blowout
   - High-freq at parity (0.95–1.0): 49.0% close vs casual 57.8% at same band — activity confound persists

*6. Extensions — next-match retention (parked)*
   - In-window next-match ≈100% for all match types — metric has no variance in a 16-day window
   - Spearman vs mean_played_next: close -0.15, neg blowout -0.01, pos blowout +0.13 — not interpretable as churn
   - Need different return definition (same-day / next-session) for a future pass

*Takeaway:* Median rolling quality healthy. Unsigned blowout cluster (~14%) in high-freq and 75k+ is real but driven by **positive blowout streaks**, not repeated stompings (neg cluster 0.06%). MM calibration looks directionally fine at parity; uncompetitive games spike when much stronger (<0.85 ratio). Park in-window retention — more datapoints coming.
