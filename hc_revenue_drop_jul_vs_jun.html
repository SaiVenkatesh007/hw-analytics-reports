Key findings

What moved

Pack softness is mix + ticket size, not fewer payers: payers flat; orders/payer −3%; rev/order −13% (~₹7.6L of the −₹9.5L).
SKU 5 & 6 dominate: −₹4.2L and −₹10.1L respectively; new SKUs 7/11/12 partially offset.
M12+ ≈ entire pack delta: M12+ pack −₹9.4L; younger cohorts mixed (M4/M2/M6 up).
Player Gacha is the attributed sink: −₹10.6L attributed (−40%) vs Currency Conv −₹1.6L. Raw HC sink also led by Player Gacha (−50%).
June gacha IAPs amplify the Player Gacha attributed hole: offer union (~276 buyers) = 37% of PG attributed-revenue decline vs only ~14% of raw gacha HC — high-ARPPU pocket. Jun-only hangover −64% attributed; Jul-only milder (−22%).
Broad geo, India largest absolute: India −₹5.2L (55% of pack drop); Tier 1/2 also soft in %.

Recommendation

One call

Treat July HC softness as mix + Player Gacha demand, with June character IAPs as a meaningful secondary cannibalization channel for M12+ whales — not the sole driver. Avoid stacking heavy Player Gacha character IAPs immediately before expecting HC/gacha pack recovery; re-check SKU 5/6 depth and M12+ offer cadence into H2.

Hypothesis

Did June Player Gacha IAPs front-load characters and hurt July HC/gacha?

Offers: 120 / 121 / 133 / 134 · June full-month buyers 214 (₹21.6L IAP) · Jul 1–15 buyers 91 (₹9.5L).
In attributed ₹, yes for exposed buyers: offer union (−₹3.6L) is 37% of Player Gacha attributed decline; Jun-exposed 34%. Raw gacha HC share was only ~13–14% — same buyers, higher ₹ weight.
Jun-only hangover: PG attributed −64% (₹4.3L → ₹1.6L); zero-gacha rate 55% → 82%; HC ARPPU −₹3.1k vs control −₹0.1k.
Jul-only contrast: PG attributed only −22% in their offer window — supports purchase-month spend, next-month pullback.
Not the whole market: control panel also −54% on PG attributed; ~56% of Jun-exposed offer buyers are M12+.
